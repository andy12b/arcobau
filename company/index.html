About ArcoBau

A Swiss construction company dedicated to quality residential building, interior finishing, and flooring — serving private clients across Switzerland with precision, reliability, and care.

Our Heritage

Rooted in Swiss craftsmanship, ArcoBau brings precision engineering and meticulous attention to detail to every project across Switzerland. Our expertise spans residential construction, interior finishing, and flooring — delivered with the reliability Swiss clients expect.

Our Mission

Construim Viitorul — We Build the Future. Our mission is to deliver high-quality construction and finishing services to private clients throughout Switzerland, combining the work ethic of skilled craftsmen with Swiss standards of quality and punctuality.

Our Values

Quality, reliability, and transparency form the core of our philosophy. We provide clear quotes, explain every stage of the work, and don't leave until everything is perfect. Written guarantee on all completed works.

Why ArcoBau

What Sets Us Apart

Own Specialist Teams

We do not subcontract. We have our own teams for each trade: masonry, tiling, flooring, drylining, and installations.

Written Guarantee

We provide a written guarantee on all work carried out. If any issue arises, we return and resolve it at no additional cost.

Swiss Punctuality

We set realistic timelines from the start and honour them. We communicate proactively about every stage of the project.

Premium Materials

We use materials from verified Swiss and European suppliers. We present the options and prices transparently — no hidden costs.
